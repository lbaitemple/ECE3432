Connection Methods

  • method 1: Mobile hotspot on Windows 11/10 or phone hotspot

    • easy, but not always reliable

  • method 2: Wireless router / PSK + MAC registration

    • easy, ROS works on WSL & robot with multicast

  • method 3: eduroam (need to generate a hash in ubuntu 18.04)

    • hard setup, universal if school enables hash authentication
    • ROS works on WSL & robot with multicast
