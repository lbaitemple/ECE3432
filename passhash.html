How to Configure Your Eduroam Connection

1 Enter your Eduroam password in the input field. The MD4 hash will be generated automatically.
2 The generated hash will automatically replace the HASH value in the configuration template.
3 Ensure your ID is a valid .edu email address and that both WiFi passwords are at least 8 characters.
4 Click "Download wireless.txt" to save your configuration file after validation passes.

About Eduroam Configuration

Eduroam (education roaming) is a secure, worldwide roaming access service developed for the international research and education community.

Your configuration requires:

  • • ID: Your institutional .edu email address
  • • HASH: MD4 hash of your password in UTF-16LE format
  • • AP: Set to "eduroam" for Eduroam networks
